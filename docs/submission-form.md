## Description

StoryCast is an autonomous multimodal AI agent that turns a single plain-English topic into a narrated one-minute short film. A user enters one idea, such as `the death of a star`, and the system automatically plans a scene-by-scene story, generates storyboard keyframes, creates narration, animates the scenes into video clips, and assembles the final film. The project is designed to make agentic execution visible: the pipeline exposes story planning, visual generation, narration, animation, and final editing as distinct steps rather than one opaque generation call. StoryCast uses the Seed model family for story generation, image generation, and video generation, with a narration layer and ffmpeg-based assembly to create a finished, submission-ready video artifact.
